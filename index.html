The ride sharing bonanza continues! Seeing the success of notable players like Uber and Lyft, everyone wants to get into the ride sharing business but where do you start? The purpose of this project was to vizualize ride sharing data by joining ride data with city data to be able to see what city type generates the largest returns

We look at average fare prices vs total number of rides to see where our high price and high volume zones which could lead to us deciding to implement a luxury line or discount codes. We also look at total fare,driver, and rides to see the distrabution between the city types.
